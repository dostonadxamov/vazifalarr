cobalt

The Chevrolet Cobalt is a compact car produced from 2004 to 2010 in North America. Since 2011, the car of the same name has been produced, focused on the markets of developing countries, but having nothing in common with the first generation. Under the Pontiac brand, the car was sold in the United States and Mexico under the G5 model in 2007-2009. The model was marketed as the Pontiac G4 in Mexico in 2005-2006 and as the Pontiac G5 in Canada.

malibu

Chevrolet Malibu (ʃəvrole maˑlibu)- a mid-range car from Chevrolet. It used to be a luxury version of the Chevrolet Chevelle, but became a separate model starting in 1978. It originally had rear-wheel drive, but when General Motors revived the Malibu in 1997, the car received front-wheel drive. Initially, the Malibu was available in North America, but since 2012, subsequent generations of the Malibu model have been sold in most global markets.
